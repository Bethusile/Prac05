If you want to be a pilot, but it is not meant to be - maybe it will help to get into the hobby of radio-controlled planes.

A radio-contolled aircraft is a small flying machine that is controlled remotely by an operator on the ground using a hand-held radio transmitter. The transmitter communicates with a receiver within the craft that send signales to servomechanisms which move the control surfaces based on the position of the joystick on the transmitter. The control surfaces, in turn, affect the orientation of the plane.
